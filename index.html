I am a Deaf, experimental particle physicist involved with the ATLAS collaboration looking for signs of new physics Beyond the Standard Model. I care about boosted object reconstruction, hadronic final states, jet substructure, pile-up mitigation techniques, the intersection of particle physics & machine learning, and more!

I am currently a 6th year graduate student at the University of Chicago. See my curriculum vitae for more information.
